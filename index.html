WE BELIEVE

ART CAN MAKE THIS WORLD A BETTER PLACE

Art Passionné Foundation is an association of artists and a non-profit organization that aims to connect patrons and lovers of art with unique, independent artists, and create a positive difference to the society. Art Passionné is a social art community for artist around the world, all fine art artist & digital artist can join Art Passionné for free and submit their artworks for sale, selected & published artworks will be available for sale. We don't take a commission from artist like other market places, But the artist can donate a portion of the sale amount to support our mission.
